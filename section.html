Adjoining Room

An adjoining room shares a wall with another hotel room and is connected by a door.

Adjacent room

An adjacent room is very close to another room but does not share a common wall with it.

Cabana

A cabana is suited away from the main hotel building, in the vicinity of a swimming pool or sea beach.

Duplex

A duplex comprises two-room situated on different floors, which are connected by an internal staircase.

Double Room

A double room has one double bed for double occupancy. The size of a double bed is 4.5feet to 6 feet.
